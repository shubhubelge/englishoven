Which English Oven loaves are suitable for vegetarians and vegans?

All EO loaves are suitable for vegetarians and vegans. EO Milk bread is made using milk solids as one of the Ingredients.

Why is there salt in bread?

Salt provides flavor to product. It reduces yeast activity. Salt has strengthening effect on the gluten.

Why is there soya in bread?

Soya is there in Bread to supplement protein that helps in better volume and crumb softness.

Do you use iodised salt?

Yes we use iodised salt in our products.

Yes, Our Plant is FSSC 22000 certified

Which English Oven products are Egg free, Nut free and Milk free?

Do your breads contain TRANS fats?

We do not use caramelized sugar in any of English Oven products.
